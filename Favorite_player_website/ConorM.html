Conor McGregor

This is a name that needs absolutely no introduction “The Notorious” is the face of the fight game and is also one of the worlds highest-paid athletes. McGregor, who hails from Ireland, was the first concurrent double champion in the UFCs history, a feat he had already emulated in his time in Cage Warriors.

What are some ofConor McGregorsbiggest accomplishments?

Conor McGregor is also the only MMA fighter to have successfully put a boxing bout of enormous proportions he fought Floyd Mayweather in 2017. Despite the loss, his name will forever be etched in record books, as “The Mystic Macs” fight is the second highest-selling combat sports PPV of all time. The fighters prime years in the UFC broke records of all kinds and made him the first-ever UFC two-division champion. The UFCs top PPV buys of all time are etched with Conor McGregors name all over it.
